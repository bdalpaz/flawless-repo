THE CLANS

LIN KUEI

An ancient clan of assassins from China. Originally noble warriors, they became corrupted and began cyberizing their members. Now led by Sub-Zero who seeks to restore their honor.

NOTABLE MEMBERS

Sub-Zero Smoke Cyrax Sektor

WHITE LOTUS

A secret society dedicated to protecting Earthrealm through spiritual enlightenment and martial arts mastery. They train the finest warriors to defend against Outworld threats.

NOTABLE MEMBERS

Liu Kang Kung Lao Bo' Rai Cho

MORTAL KOMBAT TIMELINE

THE GREAT KUNG LAO

Ancient times

The Great Kung Lao wins the Mortal Kombat tournament, defending Earthrealm from Outworld invasion for another generation.

ORIGINAL TOURNAMENT

1992

Shang Tsung hosts the 10th consecutive Mortal Kombat tournament on his island, where Liu Kang emerges victorious.

SHAO KAHN'S INVASION

1995

After Shang Tsung's failure, Shao Kahn initiates an invasion of Earthrealm, merging the two worlds.
